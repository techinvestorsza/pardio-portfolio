About Me

Embarking on my journey in the world of fashion has not only allowed me to closely collaborate with local brands, representing their unique styles and visions but has also given me a platform to share a broader mission. As an influencer, I am driven by a vision and a profound sense of purpose for the youth. My goal is to inspire and encourage them to navigate life successfully, steering clear of negative influences such as drug use, alcohol abuse, and gender-based violence (GBV).

I aspire to be a beacon of hope, showcasing that we can all overcome the challenges that come our way. My work in the fashion realm is more than just aesthetics; it's a commitment to creating impactful content, using my influence as a voice for the voiceless youth. Collaborating with local & renowned brands and exploring the dynamic fashion landscape keeps me motivated and inspired.

Thank you for being a part of my journey!
